Our Story

It started with jollof rice at the Hive in UEA. Alexis was enjoying her Nigerian jollof when Daniel appeared and declared, "Ghanaian jollof is much better."

That playful debate sparked something neither of us expected. What began as teasing over rice became long conversations, shared laughter, and a connection that felt inevitable.

Like any good recipe, love needs the right ingredients: warmth, trust, and a little spice. We're ready to begin our next chapter together.
